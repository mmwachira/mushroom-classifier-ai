Mushrooms!! Creamy Mushroom Bruschetta, Mushroom Risotto, Mushroom pizza, Mushrooms in a burger, and what not! Just by hearing the names of these dishes, people be drooling! Their flavor is one reason that takes the dish to the next level!


But have you ever wondered if the mushroom you eat is healthy for you?
From over 50,000 species of mushrooms only in North America, how will you classify the mushroom as edible or poisonous? Poisonous mushrooms can be hard to identify in the wild!


This webapp detects if a mushroom is edible or poisonous based on the specifications provided such as cap shape, cap color, gill color, etc. using different classifiers.
